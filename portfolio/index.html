Intro

Hi, I'm Leon. Thanks for looking at my portfolio! Here I am showcasing three of my playable independently developed video games. If you're mildly interested, I have other games available on my itch.io page, including platformers, word games, and horror titles. A link is provided at the bottom for all my games.

Games

The games below were coded in C# through Visual Studio and I developed them in Unity. Every asset was made from scratch, and the artwork was created using Adobe Illustrator.

Wake Him Up
Casual physics-based puzzle: Manipulate the wind to guide a falling feather through the bedroom window and wake up the procrastinator by dodging balls, kites, balloons, and moreee! The game was made to match the theme "Just Get Started".

Roundabout
Casual puzzle: Control a car that drives around silly objects, like donuts and bagels, and dodge whatever blocks your path by adjusting the speed mode with precise timing. This one is easy to start, but quite tricky to master. Also, it's mildly addictive.

Lost in Aurantius
Top-down adventure game: You find yourself on an alien planet and need to settle into the community. There are various tasks like making friends, fishing, and fruit picking. My teammate and I developed this game for the Mental Health Dev Champions 2024 jam.
